Don't Know Any Other Way To Love

May 5, 2015 . Personal . Comments
Tags: stories life

Tweet

This is a story Anand Bongir shared on Facebook. Loved it.

"So what's the plan now?"

"Plan? I am doing everything I can to get her. I have a started working at a German auto company where I have to stand for 10-hour shifts. Their process is so intensive that they make a car every 2 minutes.."

"Whoa! Every 2 minutes?! One cannot even cook Maggi in 2 minutes and they make a freaking car!?"

"Yes. They do. I've completed ITI course with distinction. I'll now get admission in a diploma college and pursue the job with full efforts and will leave no stone unturned for her family to accept me. I am even ready to change my religion for her!"

"Why?"

"Because I love her, and that is the only thing that matters. The religion I belong to, the political party I support, the God I choose to follow are not what I would choose to define me. I am ready to leave everything for her, right now, only if her family accepts me."

"Is it not too much of a sacrifice?"

"It might be, but I'm more than happy to do it. I haven't done timepass, where I see so many people change partners every week, I have loved truly and with all my heart and I do not know any other way there is to love."

I have realised that it is these people who have loved truly suffer the most and the others get off well in life. But you know what? It is all worth it."
